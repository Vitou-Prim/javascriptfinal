About Gold Steer Driving School

Gold Steer Driving is a Cambodian Driving school, based in Phnom Penh. We have many qualified & educated driving instructors. Founded in 2020, Gold Steer Driving School has more success rate than most school in the city.
Driving is an important factor for everyday life. GSDS will help you in after your own
safety and safety of others while driving.
Gold Steer driving school offer many services that will suit your need in driving, like preperation for driving exams, take driving Lessons and more!
You can find us in Khan Daun Penh, Khan Chroy Changvar and Khan Meanchey. Contact us now for more information, or you could check out our services in the page!
